About Creating Space

Creating Space is a groundbreaking Minecraft mod that seamlessly integrates with the Create mod, introducing exhilarating elements of space exploration, advanced rocketry, and interplanetary colonization to your Minecraft experience. Embark on a cosmic journey and engineer your path through the stars!

Key Features

  • Design and launch custom rockets using Create's mechanical components
  • Explore procedurally generated planets, moons, and asteroids
  • Establish and manage orbital space stations and planetary colonies
  • Research cutting-edge space technologies and uncover cosmic mysteries
  • Navigate realistic orbital mechanics and manage life support systems
  • Encounter fascinating space phenomena like nebulae, black holes, and wormholes
